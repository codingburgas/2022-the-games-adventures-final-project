Through this site you will be able to get acquainted with the rules of the game.

The rules of the game are not very complicated. You are a little black cube that you should avoid touching red cubes. By pressing "space"
you can skip them and continue the game. When the black cube touches a red cube, it says how much you have - "Your scored". If you want
to keep playing, press "Play Again?" And it starts all over again.
