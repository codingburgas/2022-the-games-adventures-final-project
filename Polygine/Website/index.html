Through this site you will be able to get acquainted with the rules of the game.

The rules of the game are not very complicated. You are a little dwarf who is in the forest and has to overcome obstacles like mushrooms
and stones without touching them, because otherwise he dies. By pressing "space" you can skip them and continue the game. When you die
he writes your record and asks if you want to keep playing.
